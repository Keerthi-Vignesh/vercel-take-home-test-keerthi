# Top 5 Favourite Tasks

1. **Work with the product team to develop a new feature based on feedback from customers: Provides an opportunity to influence product development and improve the user experience.**

2. **Identify, file (and, where possible, resolve) bugs in private and public Vercel/Next.js repos on GitHub: Directly contributes to product improvement and stability, showcasing technical expertise.**

3. **Work with engineering teams during incidents and provide updates to internal and external stakeholders: Engages in critical problem resolution and maintains transparency with stakeholders, reflecting leadership and reliability.**

4. **Write and maintain support articles and docs pages: Reduces support load by empowering customers with self-service resources.**

5. **Dig through logs to troubleshoot a customerâ€™s broken project: Involves problem-solving and directly resolving technical issues.**

# Least 5 Favourite Tasks

1. **Respond to queries on Twitter, Reddit, Hacker News, and other 3rd party sites: It can be chaotic and less efficient, often involving non-technical issues and off-topic discussions.**

2. **Engage multiple users at once in a public discussion, to answer their questions and troubleshoot problems: Handling multiple users in a public forum can be chaotic and less effective than focused, individual support.**

3. **Help resolve billing issues for customers: Billing issues are often repetitive and less technical.**

4. **Run ticket review sessions to make sure tone is consistent: This involves repetitive, administrative work rather than technical problem-solving.**

5. **Work with 3rd party partners to track down a tricky situation for a joint customer: It can be time-consuming and complicated, requiring coordination across different systems.**
